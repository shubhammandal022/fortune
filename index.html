About Our Company

It all began in 2000, with the introduction of Scaffolding unit in Punjab State . At time, when in India the market for Scaffolding was not upto the mark. We responded with fervour and proved to be a milestone in the Scaffolding industry.
We determined our journey of deliverables and achieved milestones all across the industry. We functions with a diverse range of clients worldwide, by providing superior and consistent product benefits, authenticity, and unrivalled service standards.
Pursuing the critical path with fast pace development and fineness in scaffolding industry, Fortune Group has earned a reputation among the leading manufactures of scaffolding. To attain this success in the industry, We have operated with the advanced technology, and highly skilled personnel.

Scaffolding Units in infrastructure provide safe access and support for workers during construction, repair, and maintenance of bridges, tunnels, and buildings. They ensure stability and efficiency in large-scale projects.

Casting Units are critical for shaping and forming concrete components used in construction. They are used to produce precast elements like beams, slabs, and pillars for bridges, buildings, and highways.

Forging Units are used to produce robust metal components like beams, joints, and fasteners. These forged elements ensure structural integrity and durability in bridges, buildings, and heavy machinery.
